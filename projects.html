Avionics Communications System

Designed a double-redundant communication system of VHF, UHF, and satellite transceivers, which were integrated with other RF modules, including beacons and GPS receivers.

Enabled a 15-times longer link than the previous design, capable of reaching a line-of-sight range of 15 km.

Nominated as Best Paper in class section

A performance comparison for three different antenna designs for improving the efficiency of long-range sounding rockets' transmission: the omnidirectional dipole, the microstrip patch array, and the beamforming phased dipole array antenna.
